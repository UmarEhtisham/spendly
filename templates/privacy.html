Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the app (e.g., pages visited, session duration) to help us improve the Service.

We do not collect payment information. Spendly is a free tool and does not process any financial transactions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Service:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and category breakdowns.
  • To identify and fix bugs, and to improve app performance.
  • To send important service-related communications (e.g., account security notices).

We will never use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, or deletion.

You can delete your account at any time from your profile settings. Upon deletion, all your personal data and expense records will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — to load the typefaces used in the app. Google may log font requests; see the Google Privacy Policy.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics brokers, or social media trackers.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie is strictly necessary for the Service to function and is deleted when you log out or close your browser session.

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party cookies of any kind.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, or if you have concerns about how your data is handled, please contact us using the details below.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out at support@spendly.app.
